About Me

I'm Eyasu Begashaw, a passionate architect with a degree in Architecture and extensive expertise in graphic design and motion graphics. My work blends creativity and technical precision, delivering visually stunning solutions for various institutions. From architectural designs to dynamic motion graphics, I bring ideas to life with a focus on innovation and quality.

My skills include Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects), 3D modeling (AutoCAD, SketchUp, Revit, Archicad), and more. Explore my projects to see how I transform concepts into reality.
